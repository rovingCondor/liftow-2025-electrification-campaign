Go Green with Electric Forklifts

Switching to electric forklifts reduces your environmental impact by cutting emissions and improving air quality—helping your business lead the way in sustainability.

Lower
emissions

Significantly reduce CO2 emissions by replacing propane or diesel forklifts with electric lift trucks.

Improved
air quality

Switching to electric forklifts help improve the air quality within your warehouses and enclosed spaces.

Net zero emissions
contribution

Moving to an electric fleet helps contribute to the provincial and national climate targets for 2050.

Canadian emissions
target goals

Contribute to Canada’s new 45–50% emissions reduction goal for 2035 by moving away from propane or diesel forklifts.

Operational Advantages

Reduced noise levels: Creates a quieter and more pleasant work environment.

Consistent performance: Reliable battery performance in demanding conditions.

Longer lifespan: Lithium-ion batteries and electric equipment have increased durability.

Economic Benefits of Going Electric

By choosing electric, you’ll gain environmental benefits along with savings in energy, maintenance, and available financial incentives.

Reduced energy costs

Save on operating expenses with electric forklifts that use energy-efficient power systems, lowering your overall energy consumption and costs.

Lower maintenance costs

Electric forklifts have fewer moving parts, leading to reduced wear and tear, minimized downtime, and lower long-term maintenance expenses.
